Privacy Policy

How Wildlife Home Connect collects, uses, and shares information.

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Wildlife Home Connect ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy.

Wildlife Home Connect is an independent platform designed to help homeowners identify wildlife-related issues, understand removal and exclusion approaches, and connect with independent local service providers focused on humane animal removal and property protection.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or interact with the Service.

By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the website.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit a contact or inquiry form
  • Request to be connected with a service provider
  • Communicate with us
  • Subscribe to updates or notifications

This may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • General location (city or ZIP code)
  • Description of your situation or request
  • Any additional information you choose to provide

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit the website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • IP address and approximate location
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Navigation paths and referring URLs

This information is used in aggregated or non-identifiable form where possible.

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve website functionality and understand how users interact with our content.

For more details, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and improve the website
  • Respond to inquiries and user requests
  • Facilitate connections with independent wildlife removal providers
  • Provide relevant educational and informational content
  • Analyze website performance and usage
  • Communicate updates (where permitted)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information in the following cases:

4.1 With Independent Service Providers

If you submit an inquiry, your information may be shared with independent wildlife removal providers so they can contact you regarding your request.

These providers operate independently and are responsible for their own services and data practices.

4.2 With Service Partners

We may share information with vendors supporting:

  • Website hosting
  • Analytics
  • Communication tools
  • Security and fraud prevention

These partners are required to handle data in accordance with applicable privacy standards.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Respond to lawful requests
  • Protect rights, safety, or property

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger or acquisition, information may be transferred as part of the business.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • Secure servers
  • Encryption where applicable
  • Access control
  • Monitoring for vulnerabilities

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Restrict or object to certain uses
  • Opt out of communications

To exercise your rights, contact us using the information below.

7. Children's Privacy

This website is not intended for children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If identified, such data will be removed.

8. Third-Party Links

The website may include links to external websites.

We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. Please review their policies separately.

9. California Privacy Rights

California residents may have additional rights under applicable laws, including the right to access, delete, or opt out of certain data uses.

Requests can be submitted using the contact details below.

10. International Data Transfers

Information may be processed in countries outside your place of residence.

By using the website, you consent to such transfers where permitted by law.

11. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

When no longer needed, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
